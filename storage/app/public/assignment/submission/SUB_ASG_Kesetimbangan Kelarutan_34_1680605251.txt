Diketahui Ksp Ag2CrO4 = 4 x 10-12, tentukan:

a. s di dalam air?

b. [Ag+] dalam keadaan jenuh?

c. [CrO4] dalam keadaan jenuh?

Jawab:
a. Ksp = [Ag+]2[CrO42-]

Ksp = (2s)2(s)

4 x 10-12 = 4s2

s = 10-4 mol/L

b. [Ag+] = 2s

[Ag+] = 2(10-4)

[Ag+] = 2 x 10-4 mol/L

c. [CrO42-] = s

[CrO42-] = 10-4 mol/L
